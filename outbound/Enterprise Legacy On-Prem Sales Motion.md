# OnPrem Sales Motion

**ICP:** 500+ employees x legacy on-prem infrastructure: SharePoint 2016/2019, Exchange on-prem, file servers, aging hardware.

**Primary Services:** Modern Workplace (SharePoint migration, M365), Infrastructure (tenant consolidation, Exchange migration).

---

## Buying Committee Map

| Role | Title Examples | Influence | Primary Concern | Message Angle |
|------|----------------|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Champion** | IT Director, Dir. of Infrastructure, SharePoint Admin | High — drives initiative | Ownership, drift, maintenance burden, talent retention |  =ownership + cadence |
| **Influencer** | VP IT, CISO, Finance Director | Medium–High | Cost of ownership, security posture, compliance | TCO, risk reduction, compliance |
| **Decision Maker** | CIO, CTO | Budget, final sign-off | Strategic alignment, vendor risk, change management | Outcomes, proof, referenceability |
| **Stakeholder** | Business unit leaders, HR, Legal | Medium — can block | Reliability, user experience, continuity | "No surprises" — phased rollout, training |

---

## Champion Messaging

**Who:** IT Director, Director of Infrastructure, Senior SharePoint/Exchange Admin.

**Pain:**
- End-of-life platforms (SharePoint 2016/2019, Exchange)
- Ownership - orphaned sites, permission sprawl
- Maintenance — patches, upgrades, backup
- Talent — hard to hire/replace on-prem expertise

**Message (email):**

> [Name], we work with [similar company type] who are still running SharePoint 2016/2019 or Exchange on-prem.
>

---

## Influencer Messaging

### Finance Director / VP IT

**Pain:** TCO of on-prem (hardware, licenses, maintenance, headcount).




### CISO / Security

**Pain:** Compliance, audit findings, breach risk from legacy platforms.

## Decision Maker Messaging (CIO/CTO)

**Pain:** Strategic fit, vendor risk, change management, board/council reporting.

**Message:**

> [Name], [Champion name] mentioned you’re evaluating options for [SharePoint/Exchange] modernization.
>
> We’ve run [X] similar migrations for [industry/size]. 
>
> Happy to share a short reference from [similar company] if helpful. 

---

## Stakeholder Messaging (Business Units)

**Who:** HR, Legal, dept heads: depend on SharePoint/file shares.

**Concern:** "will my stuff still work?"

**Message (internal — Champion can use):**

> We’re bringing in a partner to review our SharePoint/file share structure before we modernize. Goal: clearer ownership, less drift, and a smoother move to M365. No disruption to your day-to-day — we’ll coordinate access and timing.
