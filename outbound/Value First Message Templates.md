# 10 Value-First Outbound Messages (No Meeting Ask)

**Purpose:** Offer free value upfront. No request for a call or meeting. Build trust before any ask.

**Docs & tools:** See `docs/` for templates (3–8, 10) and analysis instructions + scripts + AI prompts (1, 2, 9). Start with [docs/README.md](../docs/README.md).

**31-day cadences:** Full free-value campaign cadences (1 template per day, 31 days, 10 campaigns) are in [value-first-campaigns/](value-first-campaigns/). Each day offers a unique free resource.

**Inbound insight:** Qualified inbound leads (550) say they're "looking for a partner," need "guidance," or "migration" help. Mirror that language — "partner," "guidance," "help you build." Top themes: SharePoint, Power Platform, migration, support. See [reports/Inbound Analysis Report.md](../reports/Inbound%20Analysis%20Report.md).

## 1. Marketing Director × Creative — Conversion Teardown

> Hi [Name] — I took 10 minutes to run your [landing page / main campaign page] through our conversion checklist. Found 3 quick fixes that usually move the needle. Sending you a one-pager with the issues + recommended changes.

---

## 2. Marketing Director × Creative — Attribution Snapshot

> [Name] — I pulled your [website / ads] and mapped where I’d expect attribution to break down. Sent you a short PDF: which channels are likely undercredited, and one change that would surface it. No strings — you can implement it yourself.

---

## 3. IT Director × Modern Workplace — Ownership Checklist

> [Name] — Here’s a simple checklist we use with teams to see which systems have clear owners vs which ones are drifting: [attached or linked]. Run through it when you have 5 minutes. If anything feels off, you’ll know where to look.

## 4. IT Director × Infrastructure — Drift Audit Template

> [Name] — Sent you a one-page template for a “systems drift” audit: what to document, who to ask, what tends to break. Use it internally or with vendors. No need to talk — just something we’ve seen work.

## 5. Director × Data & Analytics — Decision Latency Worksheet

> [Name] — Put together a simple worksheet: list your top 5 operational decisions, how long each takes to answer today, and where the delay usually sits. Use it to find the 1–2 reports that matter most. Attaching it for you to use.

## 6. Marketing VP × Marketing Services — Spend Reallocation Framework

> [Name] — We use a quick framework to see where spend leaks vs where it converts. Sent you a 1-pager: the 4 questions we ask and how we score each channel. You can run it yourself.

## 7. Finance Director × Modern Workplace — System Ownership Map

> [Name] — Here’s a blank “system ownership map” we use with finance teams: critical systems, who owns them, and how often they’re reviewed. Fill it in when you have 15 minutes. If you spot gaps, you’ll know where to tighten.

## 8. IT Director × Custom Dev — Backlog Prioritization Matrix

> [Name] — Sent you a simple prioritization matrix for dev backlogs: effort vs impact, how we decide what to push to a partner vs keep in-house. Use it as-is or adapt it. No strings.

## 9. Marketing Manager × Creative — 3 Fixes One-Pager

> [Name] — Ran your [ads / landing page] through our checklist. Here are 3 specific fixes with before/after guidance. You can hand this to your team and use it as you see fit.

---

## 10. C-level / VP × General — KPI Latency Snapshot

> [Name] — Put together a 5-question snapshot: “How long does it take to answer X?” for common operational KPIs. It helps surface where decision latency lives. Sent it over for you to use with your team.

---

## Usage Notes

- **Delivery:** Attach the doc (PDF, Excel, Notion link) or send a short link in the same message.
- **Follow-up:** If they reply with thanks or questions, you can then offer a brief call — but the first touch has no ask.
- **Segments:** Messages 1–2, 6, 9 → Marketing; 3–4, 8 → IT Director; 5, 10 → Data/Ops; 7 → Finance Director.
