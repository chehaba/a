## 5. Ten Strategic Business Questions

Use these questions for discovery calls, competitive analysis, or strategic planning. They are designed to surface revenue goals, process, and channel structure.

### Revenue Goals & Objectives

1. **What are 3Cloud’s current revenue targets (ARR, growth rate) and how do they tie to Avanade’s broader North America goals?**  
   *Probe: Annual targets, YoY growth expectations, contribution to Avanade P&L.*

2. **How is quota structured for sales—by revenue, margin, or consumption?**  
   *Probe: New logo vs. expansion mix, services vs. managed services, deal-size bands.*

### Sales Process & Motion

3. **What is the typical sales cycle (length, stages) for net-new Azure/data/AI engagements, and who is the primary economic buyer (CIO, CTO, VP Data)?**  
   *Probe: Multi-threading, approval path, procurement involvement.*

4. **How does 3Cloud balance inbound leads (Microsoft co-sell, web, events) vs. outbound prospecting, and what is the ratio of each to closed revenue?**  
   *Probe: SDR/BDR structure, lead sources, conversion rates.*

### Target Audience & ICP

5. **Who is 3Cloud’s ideal customer profile—industry, company size, Azure maturity—and how does it differ from Avanade’s broader enterprise focus?**  
   *Probe: Mid-market vs. enterprise split, vertical specialization, geographic focus.*

6. **What triggers or signals does 3Cloud use to prioritize accounts (e.g., Azure spend, Copilot pilots, data modernization initiatives)?**  
   *Probe: Intent data, Microsoft signal sharing, ABM vs. broad outreach.*

### Revenue Channels & Partnerships

7. **How does 3Cloud generate revenue through Microsoft—direct co-sell, Partner Center, CSP, or referral—and what percentage of revenue flows through each channel?**  
   *Probe: Microsoft funding, deal registration, joint GTM.*

8. **What is the relationship between 3Cloud and Avanade for deal referral, shared accounts, and conflict management?**  
   *Probe: Handoff rules, account ownership, brand usage.*

### Margins & Economics

9. **What are typical gross margins by service type (project-based vs. managed services vs. staff augmentation), and which offerings drive the highest margin?**  
   *Probe: Offshore/nearshore leverage, utilization targets, pricing model.*

10. **How does 3Cloud price engagements—fixed-fee, T&M, outcome-based—and what role does Azure consumption (usage-based revenue) play in their economics?**  
    *Probe: Consumption rebates, Microsoft incentives, revenue mix.*
