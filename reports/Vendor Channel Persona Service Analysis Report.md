## 4. Logistic Levers (Model Predictors)

**Positive (increase win odds):**
- Industry Non-Profit
- Primary Service Creative
- Function Finance
- Industry Government
- Channel Inbound/Retargeting
- Channel Customer Expansion

**Negative (decrease win odds):**
- Primary Service Data & Analytics (strongest negative)
- Channel Partners
- Primary Service Custom Dev
- Function Operations
- Industry Organizations
- Industry Law, Manufacturing, Transportation

**Implications:** Aim at Non-Profit, Government, Finance, Creative. Reposition or deprioritize Data & Analytics, Custom Dev, Partners, Operations. Avoid Organizations, Law, Manufacturing, Transportation as primary focus unless sub-segmented.

## 6. Actionable Steps

### Immediate (This Week)

1. **Double down on Account Management Campaign** — 63% win rate. Allocate more capacity to existing-customer expansion; ensure clear offer and handoff from AM to sales.
2. **Stop or fix CEM - Existing Customer New Service** — 6% win rate. Either redesign the cross-sell motion or deprioritize.
3. **Segment LinkedIn outbound** — Only run for Marketing Manager × Creative (33%) and Marketing Manager × Marketing Services (30%). Stop LinkedIn for IT, Custom Dev, Data & Analytics.
4. **Prioritize Inbound segments** — Executive/GM × Modern Workplace, Marketing Director × Marketing Services, IT Director × Custom Dev. Route leads to appropriate cadence.

### Short-Term (This Month)

5. **Build Non-Profit and Government lists** — Strong positive levers. Target with Modern Workplace, CRM, Creative messaging.
6. **Create Finance Director × Modern Workplace play** — 100% in Customer Expansion; 6 opps. Replicate conditions (existing customer, Finance ownership of systems).
7. **Reposition Data & Analytics** — Strongest negative lever. Use decision-latency and operational-control framing (per Messaging Framework); avoid generic "analytics" pitch.
8. **Audit Partners channel** — 0% win rate. Fix partner-sourced opp quality or pause.

### Ongoing

9. **Improve PPC conversion** — 177 leads, 43% win rate. Run conversion teardown; test landing page, offer, follow-up cadence.
10. **Use SEO for high-intent** — 49 leads, 45% win rate. Maintain or increase SEO investment; align content to winning segments.
