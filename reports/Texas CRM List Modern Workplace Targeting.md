# Texas CRM List — Modern Workplace / Cloud Migration Targeting

**Purpose:** Normalize job titles to Function × Seniority, then rank contacts for best chance of success in a Modern Workplace / SharePoint migration campaign.

**Campaign used:** 03 IT Director Ownership, 04 IT Director Drift, 08 IT Director Backlog (all Modern Workplace–aligned).

---

## 1. Title Normalization Rules (Specific)

| Raw Title Pattern | Function | Seniority | Modern Workplace Fit |
|-------------------|----------|-----------|----------------------|
| VP Information Systems, VP Development, CIO, CTO | IT/Tech | VP / C-level | **Highest** — owns infrastructure decisions |
| Director of IT, Director of Engineering, Director Cybersecurity, Director Operations (IT context), Delivery Engineering Director | IT/Tech | Director | **Highest** — budget owner for migration |
| Manager, IT / Manager, Business Applications / Manager, Campus Engineering | IT/Tech | Manager | **Medium** — influencer, execution focus |
| Lead/Senior Developer, Engineer, Salesforce Engineer, ServiceNow Developer, Systems Engineer, SRE | IT/Tech | IC | **Low** — champion only, needs Director intro |
| VP Marketing, Director Marketing, Manager Website & Digital Marketing | Marketing | VP/Director/Manager | **N/A** — different campaign |
| VP/Director Finance, Finance Global Executive | Finance | VP/Director | **Low** — use if IT reports to them |
| VP Business Development, VP Area, VP Talent, VP Solutions | Other | VP | **Low** — not infrastructure owners |
| Sales Manager, Account Manager, Store Manager | Other | Manager | **Skip** — no infrastructure ownership |
