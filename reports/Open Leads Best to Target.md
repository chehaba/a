## Messaging

- **Campaign:** 03 IT Director Ownership or 04 IT Director Drift
- **Hook:** "Most waste comes from 'nobody owns this system anymore.'"
- **CTA:** "Worth a 15-min check if you have any orphan systems or drift right now?"
- **Phrase:** Use "this SharePoint migration" or "this M365 consolidation" — adjust by company type.
- **Channel:** Email first, then LinkedIn (not LinkedIn-only for IT).

---

## Skip for Modern Workplace

Excluded: Business Development, Sales, Account Executive, "Member Engagement" (if customer-facing), pure Marketing (no Technology/IT in title), Licensing directors.

For those personas, use the Marketing Creative or Attribution campaign instead.
