## Takeaways

1. **Titles that work:** IT Director, Director of IT, Owner, VP, Director of Engineering, Principal Engineer, Product Owner, Product Marketing Manager, Finance roles. These own or influence Custom Dev decisions.
2. **Titles to avoid:** Principal Program Manager, Program Manager, Product Manager (especially at Microsoft/big Software) — 0% in our data.
3. **Industries that work:** Non-Profit, Government, Hospitality, Healthcare.
4. **Industries to deprioritize:** Manufacturing (0%), Insurance (0%), Software (19% — only pursue if right title).
5. **How they find us:** Personal Network, Account Management, SEO, Web Visitor convert. LinkedIn outbound for Custom Dev is weak (11%).
6. **Who comes to us:** People who find you via SEO, events, or existing relationships. Not cold LinkedIn. Frame Custom Dev as "ready-to-run team, backlog reduction" for IT Directors and Owners.
